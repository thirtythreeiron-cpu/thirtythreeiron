Our Vision

Thirty-Three Iron exists to build a community where physical strength and spiritual growth are inseparable. We're not a gym with a cross on the wall—Christ is central to everything we do.

"As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another."
— Proverbs 27:17

Christ-Centered

Weekly Bible studies, prayer rooms, and volunteer chaplains. This isn't decorative—it's foundational.

High-Intensity Training

Powerlifting platforms, squat racks, and serious equipment for serious lifters. No judgment, just iron.

Community First

You're not a membership number. You're family. We sharpen each other in body and spirit.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do you open? +

We're targeting Q3-Q4 2026 for our grand opening. Founding members will be notified first and receive exclusive access to facility tours before opening day.

Do I have to be a Christian to join? +

No! Non-Christians are absolutely welcome as long as you respect our Christ-centered culture. We're here to serve everyone seeking strength with purpose.

What makes you different from other gyms? +

We integrate faith and fitness authentically—not as decoration. With dedicated prayer rooms, weekly Bible studies, volunteer chaplains, and a focus on high-intensity strength training, we're building a community, not just a client base.

Can I cancel my founding membership? +

Founding memberships require a 6-month commitment at $40/month (covered by your $80 upfront payment). After that, you're locked at $45/month for life with month-to-month flexibility.

What equipment will you have? +

Powerlifting platforms, squat racks, bench press stations, deadlift zones, and serious free weights. We're focused on strength training—no treadmills, no spin bikes.

Where will you be located? +

We're finalizing our Oklahoma City location now in the Nichols Hills/May Avenue corridor. Founding members will receive exclusive updates as we secure the space and begin construction.

Is my $80 payment refundable? +

The $80 founding member payment is non-refundable as it secures your lifetime rate and exclusive benefits. It covers your first and last month of the initial 6-month period.
