Investor Information

Thirty-Three Iron is seeking aligned investors who believe in the mission. This is a kingdom investment — not just a financial one.

The Vision

A Gym Built on Something Real.

Thirty-Three Iron is a Christ-centered strength training facility for serious lifters in Oklahoma City. The name references the 33 years Jesus lived on earth — a built-in conversation starter for the gospel. This is not a church. It's a business run with biblical integrity, where faith shapes the culture, not the marketing.

We serve weightlifters, powerlifters, and bodybuilders who value discipline, accountability, and purpose. Christ is central, not decorative.

The Ask

$50,000 – $100,000 · A Loan, Not Equity.

We are raising a total of $50,000 – $100,000 in loan capital across multiple aligned investors. You don't need to fund the full amount — any contribution toward the total is meaningful. You are paid back with interest, and Austin & Sammi retain full ownership to steward this gym the way God intended.

Proposed Loan Terms

Structure Promissory note — fixed interest
Interest Rate 8% annual, fixed
Repayment Period 36–48 months
Payments Begin Month 13 of operations (12-month grace period)
Collateral Gym equipment serves as collateral
Ownership Zero equity given — full ownership stays with founders

Loan Forgiveness Option

At any point during the repayment period, an investor may choose to forgive part or all of their remaining loan balance as a gift to the mission. This is entirely optional and at the investor's discretion — a way to turn a financial investment into a permanent kingdom investment. Note: loan forgiveness is a personal gift and is not tax-deductible.
